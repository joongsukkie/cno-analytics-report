# CNO Creative Co — Brand Style (official)
### Source: CNO Brand Style Guide.pdf (confirmed, not guessed)

## Color palette
Terracotta is the **hero** color; sage and cream balance it. Warm, earthy, calm — never harsh.

| Role | Name | Hex |
|------|------|-----|
| Primary | Black | `#000000` |
| Primary | Burnt orange | `#996137` |
| Primary | Soft terracotta (hero) | `#db9b7f` |
| Secondary | White | `#ffffff` |
| Secondary | Sage | `#888e6f` |
| Secondary | Cream | `#fff8e7` |

**In the report generator** (tuned for on-screen legibility, all within brand):
- Page: cream `#fbf3e2` · Cards: white · Ink: `#2b2622`
- Accents: terracotta `#db9b7f`, burnt orange `#a05f34`
- Positive/up: deep sage `#5f6a4a` · Negative/down: burnt orange `#a05f34` (no jarring red — off-brand)
- Borders: soft sand `#ece2cf`

## Typography (official → free web substitutes)
The brand fonts (Quiche, Futura, Agrandir) are licensed and not web-free, so the generator uses close free equivalents:

| Use | Brand font | Free substitute (Google Fonts) | Feel |
|-----|-----------|--------------------------------|------|
| Headings | Quiche Medium | **Fraunces** | Elegant, editorial, expressive |
| Subheads/labels | Futura (caps) | **Jost** (uppercase, letter-spaced) | Structured, modern, clean |
| Body | Agrandir | **Jost** / system sans | Readable, contemporary |

*If CNO provides web-licensed brand fonts, swap the `@font-face`/link in the HTML.*

## Voice & tone (applied to the auto-narrative)
Grounded, strategic, human. **Plainspoken and direct, clear over clever, supportive not overwhelming, calm in chaos.** The generator's summary is written to sound this way — e.g. "Reach climbed 147% from May… Looking ahead — we'd suggest getting back to a steady rhythm on TikTok."

## Logo
- Primary = wax-seal mark; secondary = "cno CREATIVE CO" horizontal lockup (used in the report header/footer, cropped to `cno-logo.png`).
- Tagline: "Hand it over. We'll handle it."
- Clear space = height of the "c"; min digital width 120px; never stretch/distort.

## Graphic elements
- Core: the wax seal (authority, thoughtfulness, completion/delivery).
- Supporting: minimal, sparing social iconography — heart, ribbon/banner, arrows.
